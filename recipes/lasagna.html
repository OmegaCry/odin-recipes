Lasagna

Perfectly baked with a nice cheesy finish.

This lasagna recipe is cheesy and meaty with a subtle taste of rich spices golden brown near the corners of the tray. while the center is a light yellow.

This recipe was a Secret passed down through generations. Will leave the most picky of eaters satisfied

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • ¼ cup magarin
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix all Ingredients
  2. place in over at 350 degrees
  3. take out after 20 mins
  4. Let cool for 15 minutes
  5. Serve
