Description

This is a straight forward recipe on how to Enhance a bottle of concentrated grapefruit juice.

Ingredients

  • Concentrated grapefruit extract
  • Lime or lemon extract
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix 1 litre grapefruit extract in 2 litres of water
  2. Mix 500 millitre of lemon or lime extract with mix
  3. Add 2 table spoons of sugar
