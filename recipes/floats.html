Description

Float is used in most Caribbean Breakfasts. It can be filled with any filling of your choosing.It is light,fluffy and versitile

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Add two pounds of flour in big bowl
  2. Add half table spoon of Sugar
  3. Add half table spoon of Salt
  4. Add Yeast
  5. Add two teaspoons of Oil while mixing
  6. Add two hundred millitre of water slowly while mixing until doughy
  7. Let dough rise for 15 minutes covered with a fabric kitchen towel
  8. Place frying pain on fire with oil 2 inches deep
  9. Make small discus shapped dough and place in Oil. Flip when floats when reaady on one side
